ORGINIZATION DEDICATED TO THE WELL BEING OF HUMANITY,

EARINGS EQUALLY AMONG THEMSELVES.

XII (LUDVIG JACOB THE TWELTH) AS THE FIRST MEMBER OF
THE STEN GROUP.
