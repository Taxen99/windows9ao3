We are Fanfactions.net - an Online Webservice dedicated to democratizing Fanfiction and Fandom for the masses

Our GOALS

  1. To Give Fanfiction to the masses
  2. To help YOU find your faction in this tangled world of fandoms
  3. To abolish the outdated copyright system, ushering in an era of unprecedented fandom wealth
  4. To support new creators within thriving factions, giving the gift of community to everyone
  5. To decrease petty arguments over insignificant issues

To accomplish these terms, we are investing in the Internet, as a means of distribution. It is both:

  • A promising means to bring publishing to the masses, while eliminating the, frankly evil, middlemen; and
  • A convenient legal gray zone, allowing You and us to write as we please, without interventions
